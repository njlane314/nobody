********
Glossary
********

actor
   The human, CI job, service account, or organization principal that starts a
   run.

subject
   The command, process tree, agent, MCP server, or tool being executed.

capability
   A typed grant of authority over a resource or operation.

policy
   The reviewable declaration of authority for a run.

decision
   The result of evaluating a request against policy: ``allow``, ``deny``, or
   ``ask``.

trace
   Append-only event evidence describing a run.
